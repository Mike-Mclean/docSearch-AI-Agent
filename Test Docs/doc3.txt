Client Fee Schedule

The advisory fee is calculated as a percentage of assets under management and is billed quarterly in arrears.

The annual fee schedule is as follows:
- 1.00% on the first $1,000,000
- 0.75% on assets between $1,000,001 and $5,000,000
- 0.50% on assets above $5,000,000

Additional fees may apply for specialized financial planning services, including estate planning and tax optimization.
